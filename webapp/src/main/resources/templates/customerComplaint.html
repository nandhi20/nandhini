FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the fair speed usage limit in case of unlimited data plan?

This is the limit of data consumption upto which the subscriber get the promised speed. After the limit get exhausted, the speed is reduced. TRAI has mandated the Telecom Service Provider clearly specifying their fair usage limit and the data speed to be provided after the fair usage limit gets exhausted.

What is the minimum download speed prescribed for broadband?

As per quality of service of broadband service (Second Amendment) regulations, 2014 a minimum download speed of 512 kbps will be provided to an individual subscribers by the VoizFonica.

Why I am not getting promised speed after changing my plan?

It takes about 2 hours post activation for you to get the revised speed. If it has been over 2 hours, reboot your router. If the problem still persists call 191 for further assistance.

I want to know about Aadhaar Based Verification process

To verify your mobile number with Aadhaar, you may call 191 or visit our nearest VoizFonica Store with your Aadhaar number.

How can I know my data usage?

Simply login at VoizFonica.com and go to Usage section to view your data usage. Go to My Account >> Data Balance to view your data usage.

How can I change my bill plan?
